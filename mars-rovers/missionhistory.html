Curiosity Rover

Launch & Landing

  • Launch: 2011
  • Landing: 2012 in Gale Crater

Mission Goals

  • Study Mars' climate and geology
  • Determine if Mars could support life
  • Search for organic compounds

Major Discoveries

  • Evidence of ancient lakes
  • Organic molecules in Martian soil

Perseverance Rover

Launch & Landing

  • Launch: 2020
  • Landing: 2021 in Jezero Crater

Mission Goals

  • Search for signs of ancient microbial life
  • Collect rock and soil samples
  • Test technology for future human missions

Major Discoveries

  • Evidence that Jezero Crater once had water
  • High-quality rock core samples
