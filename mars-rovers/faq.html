Common Questions

Why does NASA send rovers to Mars?

To study the planet and search for signs of past life.

How do rovers communicate?

They send signals through orbiters back to Earth.

What powers the rovers?

Curiosity and Perseverance use nuclear-powered RTGs.

How long does a message take to reach Mars?

Between 5 and 20 minutes depending on distance.
